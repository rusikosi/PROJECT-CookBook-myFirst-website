About my friend

When I was a teenager, my family was very poor. I needed pocket money, so I found a summer job as an assistant cook.
I worked for a summer in a restaurant and I really liked it.
The chef was a woman, she was very strict and knew everything about cooking, I admired her.
That's how I decided to get a chef's degree and tie myself to cooking forever
